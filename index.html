Instructions:

Gameplay:

  • Simon will blink one of the colored buttons and play a corresponding sound.
  • Repeat the sequence by pressing the same button.
  • Simon will then repeat the original sequence and add one more light and sound to the end.
  • Repeat the new, longer sequence by pressing the buttons in the same order.
  • Each time you successfully repeat the sequence, Simon will add one more step to the sequence.

Progress:

  • Continue repeating and adding to the sequence as long as you can.
  • The sequences become progressively longer and more challenging.

Losing the Game:

  • If you press a button out of sequence, Simon will emit a buzzer sound indicating the game is over.
